# GLOSSARY OF FIPS COMPLIANT TERMINOLOGY

## 1. NIST 800-53 Control Mappings

- **AC-1**: Access Control Policy and Procedures - Policies to manage access control mechanisms.
- **AC-2**: Account Management - Processes and policies for account creation and management.
- **AU-2**: Audit Events - Specifications of events that need to be audited.

## 2. Cryptographic Algorithm Terminology

- **AES**: Advanced Encryption Standard - A symmetric encryption algorithm.
- **SHA-256**: Secure Hash Algorithm 256 - A cryptographic hash function.

## 3. Zero-Trust Architecture Terms

- **Zero Trust**: A security concept that requires all users to be authenticated and authorized.
- **Micro-Segmentation**: The practice of splitting networks into smaller, manageable parts.

## 4. Audit and Forensics Vocabulary

- **Audit Trail**: A detailed log of actions performed within a system.
- **Chain of Custody**: Documentation of the handling of evidence.

## 5. Supply Chain Risk Management Terms

- **Third-Party Risk**: The potential for loss associated with third-party vendors.
- **Supply Chain Disruption**: Interruptions in the flow of goods or services.

## 6. Government Compliance Frameworks

- **FISMA**: Federal Information Security Management Act - A U.S. law aimed at protecting government information.
- **FIPS**: Federal Information Processing Standards - U.S. standards that specify security requirements.
