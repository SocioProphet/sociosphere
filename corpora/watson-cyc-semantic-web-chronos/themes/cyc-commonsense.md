# Cyc, Commonsense, and Governed Knowledge Acquisition

Status: theme synthesis; source and rights normalization incomplete.

## Core shift

The Cyc lineage treats intelligence as a durable knowledge-infrastructure problem: explicit assertions, contexts, defaults, exceptions, microtheories, inference, and governed acquisition.

The lesson is not to copy Cyc as a closed ontology. The lesson is to build a source-tracked, reviewable, open, contextual knowledge-growth loop.
